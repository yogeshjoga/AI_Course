Q1. Which phase of the SDLC focuses on defining the exact scope and requirements of the product?

A. Implementation
B. Requirements Analysis & Planning
C. Testing
D. Deployment

Answer:

✅ B. Requirements Analysis & Planning

Explanation:

Planning and requirements analysis determine product scope, feasibility, and student/customer needs.

---

Q2. What is the core principle of "Product Thinking"?

A. Writing code as fast as possible without tests.
B. Focusing on the user's real problems and finding the right features to solve them.
C. Designing database partitions first.
D. Copying competitors' features blindly.

Answer:

✅ B. Focusing on the user's real problems and finding the right features to solve them.

Explanation:

Product thinking starts with understanding user pain points and designing solutions that add business value.

---

Q3. Why must developers understand the business problem before choosing an AI model?

A. The choice of architecture, constraints, and latency depends on what the business is trying to achieve.
B. Business problems dictate GPU physical hardware assembly.
C. Without a business problem, AI models cannot run compilation scripts.
D. Models require business documents as standard training input.

Answer:

✅ A. The choice of architecture, constraints, and latency depends on what the business is trying to achieve.

Explanation:

You must map technical features directly to business constraints (like latency, budget, and accuracy) to design effective systems.
